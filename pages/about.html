About PNW Trails

Meet Evan Holt

Growing up in the shadow of the Cascade Mountains, Evan Holt developed a deep love for the Pacific Northwest wilderness at an early age. What started as weekend family hikes turned into a lifelong passion for exploring and protecting the region's incredible trail systems.

After noticing an increase in trail damage and unsafe practices during the outdoor recreation boom, Evan founded PNW Trails in 2024 with a simple mission: make trail information accessible while promoting responsible recreation.

"I believe everyone should have the opportunity to experience the beauty of our trails," says Evan. "But with that opportunity comes responsibility — to ourselves, to other trail users, and to the land itself."

Our Mission & Values

Accessibility

Providing clear, accurate trail information that helps everyone find their perfect outdoor adventure, regardless of experience level.

Stewardship

Educating trail users about Leave No Trace principles and responsible recreation to preserve our wilderness for future generations.

Community

Building a community of outdoor enthusiasts who support each other and work together to protect the trails we love.

Our Commitment

PNW Trails is committed to providing up-to-date, accurate information about trails throughout the Pacific Northwest. We regularly update our trail reports with current conditions, seasonal considerations, and safety information.

We partner with local land management agencies, volunteer trail maintenance groups, and outdoor education organizations to ensure our information is reliable and our message of responsible recreation reaches as many people as possible.

Every trail featured on our site includes not just directions and difficulty ratings, but also guidance on how to minimize your impact and maximize safety. Because the best outdoor experience is one that's safe for you and protective of the environment.
