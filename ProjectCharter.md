## **1\. Project Purpose / Background**

The purpose of this project is to design and develop a user-friendly hiking website focused on the Pacific Northwest. Many hikers struggle to find accurate, centralized, and easy-to-understand trail information. Existing websites are often outdated, confusing, or focused more on advertising than safety and responsible outdoor use.

This project aims to provide a clear and reliable website that helps hikers plan trips safely while also encouraging environmental responsibility and respect for nature.

## **2\. Project Objectives**

**Objective 1:**  
 Create a clear and easy-to-navigate hiking website.

**Objective 2:**  
 Provide essential trail information such as difficulty level, distance, elevation, and safety tips.

**Objective 3:**  
 Promote responsible hiking practices and environmental awareness.

### **Success Criteria**

* The website meets all project requirements

* Information is accurate and easy to understand

* The project is completed on time

* All group members contribute equally

## **3\. Project Scope**

### **In Scope**

* Website layout and structure

* Presentation of trail information

* Safety and responsibility guidelines

* Basic front-end website development

### **Out of Scope**

* Mobile application development

* Real-time GPS tracking

* Paid features or advertisements

* Advanced backend systems or databases
